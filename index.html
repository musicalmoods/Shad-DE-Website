Our Mission

Tackling transportation challenges for a sustainable future.

The Problem

Youth in Canada face significant barriers to using public transportation. High costs, unreliable service, and a general stigma lead many to choose car-centric options. This contributes to financial strain, environmental damage, and social isolation in sprawling suburban areas where services are far apart.

Our Solution

To combat this, we've designed a unique and powerful incentive system. TapTrack rewards you for using public transit, turning every trip into an opportunity. By earning points, you can redeem rewards ranging from discounts at local businesses strengthening our communities, to collectible, special-edition RFID tags shaped like transit vehicles.

This system gamifies transit, making it fun and engaging. The increased ridership generates revenue that can be reinvested into the transit system, funding new routes and service improvements. It's a cycle of positive change, making transit the smarter, more appealing choice for everyone.

Potential Impact

Our goal is to shift the primary mode of youth transportation from cars to public transit. This change fosters more sustainable habits, reduces carbon emissions, and offers a cost-effective alternative that eases financial burdens. By increasing demand, we can help build a more robust, reliable, and accessible public transit network for all Canadians, connecting communities and creating a greener future.
